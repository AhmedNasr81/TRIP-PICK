small discriptio
Tourism Marketplace — What the App Is
A platform where tourists discover and explore tour programs offered by travel companies, similar in spirit to a simplified TripAdvisor or Airbnb Experiences.

The Tourist Experience
When a tourist signs up and logs in, they can:
Browse & Discover
Browse a catalog of tour programs from various companies. Each program card shows the name, cover image, country, price, duration, start date, the company offering it, and an average star rating of the company that manage it,  because the program is not having a rate . The listing is fully filterable — by country, price range, duration range, date range, and sortable by price, duration, rating, or newest and other filter stuff exsist in the apis.
Program Detail Page
Clicking a program opens a full detail view that includes a main cover image, a gallery of additional photos, a full description, the duration (in days), start date, price, the company name and country it belongs to, and the average rating. There's also a heart/favorite button to save it.
Favorites
Tourists can save any program to their favorites list and come back to it later. They can also remove programs from favorites. It's essentially a personal wishlist.
Reviews
Tourists can leave a review on a company — a star rating (1–5) and a written comment (up to 1000 characters). They can only review each company once. They can later edit or delete their own review.
Profile
Tourists have a profile with their first name, last name, email ...etc, and a profile picture they can upload. They can update their name, change their password, or upload a new photo anytime ....etc.

The Company Experience
Companies have their own side of the platform:
Company Profile
After registering, a company sets up a profile with a name, description, physical address, WhatsApp number (the primary way tourists contact them) .... etc , and an optional additional contact. They can upload a logo/banner image. Their profile is publicly visible with their average rating and total number of reviews.
Managing Programs
Companies create and manage their own tour programs. Each program has a name, description, country, price, duration, start date, a main cover image, and a photo gallery (multiple images). They can activate or deactivate a program to control its visibility on the platform. They can edit or delete their programs at any time.
Reviews
Companies can see all the reviews tourists have left for them, along with the reviewer's name and photo.

The Admin Experience

Admins have a dashboard and full control over the platform:
Dashboard Stats
A stats overview showing total users, tourists, companies, programs, countries, reviews, and favorites across the entire platform.
User Management
Admins can browse all users, search and filter by role or active status, view any user's full profile, activate or deactivate any account, or permanently delete a user.Country Management
Admins manage the list of countries that programs and companies can be tagged with. Each country has a name and a cover image (used as destination cards when tourists browse by country). Admins can add, edit, delete countries, and upload their images.
Company Oversight
Admins can see and browse all company profiles on the platform.

Connecting Tourists and Companies
There's no in-app booking or payment. When a tourist finds a program they like, they contact the company directly via the WhatsApp number on the company profile. The platform is a discovery and trust layer — companies build credibility through their program listings, photos, and review ratings, and tourists use that to decide who to reach out to.

In Short
Think of it as a tour program directory with a review system and a favorites list. Companies list their trips with photos and details, tourists browse and save what interests them, reviews build trust, and contact happens off-platform via WhatsApp.
